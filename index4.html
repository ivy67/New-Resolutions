Education

Education has been looking different since last year and even in 2021. The COVID-19 pandemic has changed education for all of us and pushed many into virtual learning. Here are some tips to improve your virtual learning experince for the 2021 year.

  • Reach out to teachers when you need help.
  • Find a way to organize your work. Tools such as Notion and Google Calender are great!
  • Cut down on the procrastination.
  • Work on unfinished work in free time so it won't be a concern later.
