Health

During these unprecented and uncertain times, our health is more important than ever. Below are some health goals and resolutions to help you get through this year.


Mental Health

Mental health is important everyday. It affects a lot of what we do. Here are some ways to maintain your mental health this year:

  • Reach out to friends and family members you haven't talked in a while!
  • Take a day off for yourself and practice some self care.
  • Spend some time outside and connecting with nature.
  • Reach out for help if you need.

Physical Health

Physical health is just as important as mental health, so here are some resolutions to mainitan good physical health this year.

  • Get enough hours of sleep each night!
  • Eat more healthy foods such as fruits and vegetables.
  • Drink more/enough water everyday.
  • Take some time off for yourself and practice self are, this can include yoga or mediation.
